About

I am a doctoral student (3rd year) at the Graduate School of Culture and Information Science, Doshisha University. My research interests include Natural Language Processing, multi-task learning, document classification, and keyphrase extraction.

I have received fellowships such as the SPRING program (2023–2024) and the Fellowship for Young Researchers (2022–2023). I also joined an internship at NTT Communication Science Laboratories in 2023.
